Activities at Camp

Sports offered at Camp

There are seceral sports offered at Camp. Campers typicallly choose one to focus on with the option of sampling the others. The sports offered are listed below.

  • Swimming
  • Gymnastics
  • Tennis
  • Soccer

Other Activities at Camp

In addition to the sports listed above Acorn Summer Camp offers numerous other activities to keep campers days full of fun and advanture. Some of the activities are listed below/

  • Boating
    • Boating options include canoes, patoons and speed boats. Water tubing and water skiing are popular activities.
  • Cook-outs
    • Cook-outs are festive events that campers typically enjoy about once or twice per session.
  • Hiking
    • With over 15 miles of hiking trails there is no shortage of what campers may discover.
  • Rock Climbing
    • An indoor rock climbing facility provides opportunities for begginers through advanced level climbers.
  • Arts and Crafts
    • Our arts and crafts center offers many ways for campers to explore their creativity.
  • Outdoor Group Games
    • Outdoor games include capture the flag, savenger hunts and more!
  • Fishing
    • Acorn Lake is filled with many speices fish making fishing a popular activity at camp
