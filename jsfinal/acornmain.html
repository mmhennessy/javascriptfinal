Acorn Summer Sports Camp

General Information about Camp

Acorn Summer Camp is located near Ely, MN. It is designed for adventurous and athletic boys and girls between the ages of 8 and 15. The camp runs each summer between mid-June and mid-August. While Acorn Summer Camp is officially a sports camp where campers can choose between several sports to focus on there are also many other adventures and activities for campers to enjoy.

Quick Facts About Camp

  • We serve approximently 1,400 campers every summer.
  • Camp can accomodate 350 campers at one time.
  • Camp has state of the art sport facilities.
  • Acorn is has more than 15 miles of hiking trails.
